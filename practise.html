Greg Lemond

An American Champion

From humble beginings in Carson City, Nevada young Greg would go on to conquer the most intensive sporting event on the planet, Le Tour de France. In October 1978 he wrote a list of goals that most would think unachievable. He accomplished all ,but one. The 1980 Olympics were not possible to attend because of the boycott. He did however become a Jr.World Road Champion,a World Road Champion, and a Tour de France winner.
